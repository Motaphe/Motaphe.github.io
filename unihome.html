    As a part of the HackATL hackathon we 4 strangers from 4 different countries, 4 different universities, and 4 different majors came together to build a business prototype regarding student apartment and house subleasing in 48 hours and pitched it to judges, angel investors, and venture capitalists.

We implemented the UI/UX design using Figma, the Frontend using HTML/CSS, and the Backend using PHP and MySQL where we also implemented the k-Nearest Neighbors Algorithm for roommate matching.
